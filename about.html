location & History("Haighway par Swagat Hai")

Situated near Nandpur village in Surajgarha block, Lakhisarai district, right next to the bustling highway, Jagdamba Hotel has been serving happiness on plates for over a year. Our dhaba-style, family-friendly restaurant welcomes everyone — from long-haul truck drivers to families and travellers passing through the road.

Food Specialty & Atmosphere ("Sirf Asli Indian Swad ")

We specialize in authentic Indian food, cooked with fresh ingredients and a whole lot of love. Whether you’re here for a quick breakfast or a hearty lunch, our aim is simple — to serve you the best-tasting food that makes you feel at home.
